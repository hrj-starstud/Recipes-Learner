PIZZA PARANTHA

INGREDIENTS
# chapati (as many as you want)
# whichever sauce you want (best results with tomato)
# cheese (mozzarella preferred)
# whatever toppings you want
# microwave
Method:
1) spread the sauce on the chapati
2) sprinkle the cheese on top
(do the same again if you want another layer)
3) put it into the microwave for 2 - 2:30 minutes
4) enjoy!

PANEER CHEESE PAKORAS

INGREDIENTS
10 to 12 brown bread slices
200 gram paneer, grated
1 teaspoon ginger, grated
2 tablespoon mint leaves, finely chopped
2 tablespoon coriander leaves, finely chopped
Method:
In a mixing bowl, combine together grated paneer, ginger, mint, coriander, chilli flakes, salt and pepper.
Mix them all well.
Keep the paneer mixture aside.
To prepare the paneer bread pakora, take one rolled bread slice, place a tablespoon of the above paneer mixture slightly towards one of the edges of the slice.
Brush the other edges with little water to help sealing of the roll.
Gently and tightly seal the edges
Fry the rolls in oil of medium flame.

WHITE SAUCE PASTA

INGREDIENTS
Pasta : 1 cup
Capsicum :1 cup (finely chopped)
French beans : 10 to 12 (finely chopped)
Carrots : 1
Baby corn : 4
Milk : 300 liter
Refined flour : 2 tbsp
Butter : 2 to 3 tbsp
Cream : cup
Oil :2 tbsp
Method:
Heat 3 cups of water in any vessel.
Add tsp salt and 2 tsp oil into it.
After it starts simmering, place pasta into it and let it boil.
Boil the pasta for 10 minutes and stir it.

Strain the pasta in a sieve to drain the excess water.
Add all ingredients.
